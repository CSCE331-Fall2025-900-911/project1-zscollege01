About Me

Howdy!

I'm Zaahir Sharma, a Junior Computer Science student at Texas A&M University. I'm a passionate full-stack developer that thrives on bringing ideas to life, from the first line of code to the final deployment.. My specialties include Python, Java, and React/React Native and I actively dabble in AI and machine learning projects. I am currently working on several personal projects that leverage AI to solve problems that resonate with me. Most recently, I have been working on an AI-powered web application to help programmers prepare for behavioral interviews. But, in the near future I intend on creating an AI optimized social media that gives users a tailored social experience.

My software development journey began in elementary school when I built and programmed a small robot for my robotics club. Those years sparked the fire that drives me to create digital solutions for real life problems. Through out the rest of my education, I found myself gravitating to college-level programming classes and club like the Robotics Club and Technology Student Association. I was always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow as a developer, so with my first internship I leveraged Google Sheets and Forms to create a donation management system for a South American charity organization. Since then, I have worked on a variety of projects, from web development to computer vision with machine learning.

When I'm not coding, you can find me playing basketball, lifting at the gym, or binge watching Netflix K-Dramas. I am also an avid music lover, so I always have my headphones on listening to everything EXCEPT country music. After I finish my work for the day, I like to unwind by playng video games like Madden, FIFA, NBA2K, and Rocket League. Latest Project: Synapse

This is an AI-powered web application designed help up-and-coming programmers prepare for behavioral interviews. Powered by Google Firebase for registered users, this webapp leverages Google Gemini for natural language processing and Vapi AI for generating interview questions and feedback utilizing text-to-voice. Overall, this is a sleek and modern webapp that showcases my skills in full-stack development and AI integration.

Favorite Project: AI Resume Critiquer

This is an AI-powered web application designed to help users improve their resumes. Utilizing Python, Streamlit, and OpenAI's GPT-4o-mini API, this app provides personalized feedback and suggestions to enhance resume content and format. The user-friendly interface allows users to easily upload their resumes and receive instant critiques, making it a valuable tool for job seekers looking to optimize their applications.
